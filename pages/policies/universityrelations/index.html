University Relations and Compliance Policy

Approved and written by the UMKL Board - Last updated 04/11/2025

Purpose and Overview

The University Mario Kart League (UMKL) exists to provide a friendly and competitive environment for students studying at a university level in the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. It is an independent, volunteer-run, non-commercial community project created by students, for students and run by students.


Non-Affiliation and Non-Commercial

The University Mario Kart League (UMKL) is not affiliated with or endorsed by any university, students' union, students' society or educational institution. We instruct our prospective teams to avoid using any name or branding affiliation with their respective institution of study. Participation by university students, alumni or faculty is strictly voluntary and informal, and should not require any external membership or fee (i.e. society membership) to participate in.

The University Mario Kart League is a fan-run and non-commercial community competition run online using Nintendo-operated online servers. We are not affiliated with Nintendo. We do not collect funds, sell merchandise, or seek sponsorships from corporations, educational institutions or charitable organisations.

The University Mario Kart League acknowledges that Mario Kart and all related trademarks, game assets and logos are the property of Nintendo Co., Ltd. All gameplay is used and published within the scope of fair, non-commercial, fan-led community engagement. The UMKL does not claim ownership of any Nintendo intellectual property.

Duty of Care and Safeguarding

The University Mario Kart League is an independent student-run online community. Participation is voluntary and outside the scope of university liability. Competitors are encouraged to read our Code Of Conduct and may face removal from UMKL activities if found to be breaching our rules.

Our Code Of Conduct, which promotes sportsmanship and inclusivity, is public on our website. Breaches of conduct, including harassment, hate speech, or generally disruptive behaviour, will result in disciplinary action or removal from UMKL activities. The UMKL welcomes competitors from all backgrounds and identities. Discrimination or exclusion on the basis of race, gender, sexuality, disability, religion, or nationality is strictly not tolerated.

The University Mario Kart League is open only to individuals aged 18 or above who are enrolled in, or alumni/faculty of, higher educational institutions within the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. This ensures compliance with online safeguarding expectations and reduces institutional risk.

The University Mario Kart League is coordinated by a volunteer board made up of students and alumni from across the United Kingdom. The board oversees rules, moderation, and season management. Participants may report misconduct, breaches of conduct, or concerns regarding fairness or safety through the UMKL Board on discord directly. All reports are handled by neutral and non-affiliated board members, ensuring equal treatment and clarity.

Data Protection and Privacy

The UMKL stores Discord user IDs only for match organisation and point tracking purposes. This is stored safely and securely in accordance with the Data Protection Act (2018) and is not disclosed in any capacity without the consent of the owner.

Discord user IDs and match records are retained for the duration of the active season, and for results archiving only. Closing Words

The University Mario Kart League wants to ensure that every student has an amazing experience with us, and we can't wait to see you on the track. For queries, feedback or concerns, please contact the UMKL Board through our Discord server.
