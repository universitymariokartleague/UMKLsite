Season 2 Round 1 Fixtures Revealed!

01/10/2025 | Season 2
Written by skjamm
Edited by zy

The first round of Season 2 fixtures have been revealed, and we are kicking things off in style. In the coming weeks we will see 6 matches fought between 12 universities across the UK, and all of these will be live on the UMKL YouTube channel for you to enjoy!

Click here or watch the video below to see watch matches will take place in round 1!

Teams will compete in these matches in October.

York VS BIMM

Our Season 1 title holders York are drawn against the oldest of newcomer teams BIMM. These teams last played a test match together in June, which fell in York's favour by a score of 516 - 468. Will York carry their momentum forward, or will BIMM upset the champions?

Keele VS Uosheff

Uosheff might fancy their chances against Keele, who rounded off the table in their last outing. Keele last played a match in April, with a loss to their local rivals in Staffs, while Uosheff are yet to play a competitive fixture. Who will come out on top in this clash of underdogs?

Glasgow VS Staffs

The runners up from Season 1 Staffs will be playing Glasgow in a chaotic first round tie. These teams have met once before, back in April with a victory in favour of Staffs. Can Glasgow bite back, or will Staffs continue their good run of form?

Liverpool VS Birmingham

Our first clash of the newcomers sees Liverpool play Birmingham in a tantalising competitive debut. Birmingham have chalked up impressive results in test matches recently, against Warwick and Cambridge, but can Liverpool catch them by surprise in their first ever match?

Brighton VS Edinburgh

The newest addition to the UMKL roster, Brighton, will face Season 1 veterans Edinburgh in their debut outing. With Edinburgh last playing back in February, Brighton could certainly test the waters and snatch a hard fought result. A fun watch is definitely in store with this fixture, but which team can take home the win?

Cambridge VS Warwick

A blockbuster tie sees Cambridge, with an upset win against York in their last match, against a red hot Warwick side in an action packed match, where the winner takes all. Which of these new teams will make their mark on an incredible first round in the new season?

Best of luck to all our teams, and may the best team win!
