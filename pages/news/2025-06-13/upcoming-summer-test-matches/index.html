Upcoming summer test matches!

13/06/2025 | Intro
Written by zy

More test matches between teams are being planned throughout the weeks as exams finish and summer approaches.

Please check the matches page to see confirmed and upcoming matches.

Now is also a good time for board members to liaise with other universities and plan test matches with each other, especially if your team hasn't had a Test Match yet.


A match graphic, as seen in the events tab on the Discord server.

These continued test matches further test the Cheep Cheep application and YouTube streaming. Members of the relevant teams can sign up to compete in this match using the button in the team's announcement channel.

These matches will be streamed on the UMKL YouTube channel. You can watch upcoming livestreams here.
