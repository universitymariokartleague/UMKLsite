Join the UMKL Discord server!

19/04/2025 | Intro Discord
Written by skjamm, untitledgoosegame.
Edited by zy

The University Mario Kart League Discord server is the beating heart of our operations.

From match organisation, to game results and general UMKL activity, the Discord server is the fastest way to keep up to date with the UMKL.

The server is also a thriving hub for all UMKL news, and is a great way to connect with other players and make friends across the UK.


With the Cheep Cheep app, users can fetch player and match data in the server.

Featured in our Discord server is our bespoke app - Cheep Cheep, ready to provide data about players in the UMKL, stats about teams. It can also manage a team's current roster before each match, and generate sleek custom images after every match, showing a team and player's score, along with the standings for a current season.
The Cheep Cheep app was developed by Adeola Adeniji, zy and Tushar Sundarka, please visit the credits for all UMKL credits.

It's also loaded with a bunch of tools helping board members manage matches and teams. This all ensures smooth running of the league.

The server is also the quickest way to contact the UMKL board, who are happy to answer any of your questions and queries. This is helpful for upcoming teams and universities who might be interested in taking part in future seasons.
Please visit Information for universities wanting to join for more information on this topic.

Whether you're a player, a committee member or a fan, come join our Discord server and be a part of our lovely community!
