Team Guidelines

Rules and guidelines for new teams wanting to join the UMKL

Important
Please make sure you have fully read the Joining Guide for information about joining the UMKL as a new team.

Name

The name by which your team will be known in the UMKL

  • Team names must be 16 characters or less
  • Do not use numbers or special characters
  • Choose a simple name that your university is recognised by. For example, a suitable name for the University of Staffordshire's team would be "Staffs", as this is what the university is colloquially known as

Colour

Colour is an important part of a team's identity

  • Your colour must be visible across both dark and light modes on Discord, as it will be used as your team's Discord role colour and as an accent colour across UMKL graphics

Please use this colour picker to see your colour across both dark and light modes on Discord.

  • Must be used in the command as a hex colour code
  • Feel free to use your university's official colours, just make sure they are visible on Discord
  • Colour cannot be pure white or pure black
  • Your colour may be slightly altered by board members if necessary

Emblem

Your team will be represented through your emblem, this does not need to be submitted immediately upon your team's creation, but will need to be submitted before your team's first match

  • The team should submit their own emblem, which will be used for all graphics in the league
  • It should be of a 1:1 (square) ratio, high resolution and submitted as a .png file (with transparency)
  • Should represent your university, team name, or location rather than just Mario or Nintendo, so each team has a distinct, recognisable identity
  • Must not be similar to other team's emblems
  • Your emblem may be altered by board members if necessary
Info
