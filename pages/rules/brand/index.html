Social Media

Guidelines for posting our content online

Important
Promotion of the UMKL via separate society channels is allowed, but a distinction must be made about how you do not need to be part of that society to be part of the UMKL team. Please read the Brand Guidelines page for more details about how you can use UMKL intellectual property and content.

Please see the below points for specific information.
  • Please do not reupload full unedited matches from the UMKL channel onto separate society/personal channels. Clips and montages/highlight reels are OK and encouraged!
  • A desclaimer should be included in the description, which could go along the lines of:
    "This is a match/clip/content from the UMKL. Anyone who is a member of any of the universities playing in the UMKL can participate. You do not need to be a member of this society to join your university's UMKL team."
  • Please leave a link to https://umkl.co.uk and the Discord server if you do upload content related to the UMKL
  • Please do not obscure or edit information on our graphics. For example: cropping out the test match watermark in graphics uploaded to social media
