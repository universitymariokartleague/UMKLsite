University Relations and Compliance Policy

All university teams are independent and not affiliated with any university, union or brand

Purpose and Overview

The University Mario Kart League (UMKL) exists to provide a friendly, competitive environment for students studying at university level in the United Kingdom. It is an independent, non-commercial community project, created and run by students, for students.

Non-Affiliation and Non-Commercial

The UMKL is not affiliated with or endorsed by any university, students' union, students' society or educational institution. Teams are instructed to avoid using any name or branding that implies affiliation with their institution - see our Team Guidelines and Brand Guidelines for details. Participation by university students, alumni or faculty is strictly voluntary and informal, and should not require any external membership or fee, such as a society membership.

The UMKL is a fan-run, non-commercial community competition played online using Nintendo's servers. We are not affiliated with Nintendo. We do not collect funds, sell merchandise, or seek sponsorship from corporations, educational institutions or charities.

The UMKL acknowledges that Mario Kart and all related trademarks, game assets and logos are the property of Nintendo. All gameplay is used and published within the scope of fair, non-commercial, fan-led community engagement, and the UMKL does not claim ownership of any Nintendo intellectual property.

Duty of Care and Safeguarding

The UMKL is an independent, student-run community, and participation is voluntary and outside the scope of university liability. All competitors are expected to follow our rules and code of conduct, which promote sportsmanship and inclusivity. Breaches, including harassment, hate speech or generally disruptive behaviour, may result in disciplinary action or removal from UMKL activities.

The UMKL welcomes competitors from all backgrounds and identities. Discrimination or exclusion on the basis of race, gender, sexuality, disability, religion, or nationality is strictly not tolerated.

Our code of conduct is not limited to official UMKL matches and channels. Off-platform behaviour, such as conduct in direct messages, other Discord servers, or otherwise in person, may still result in disciplinary action or removal from UMKL activities if it breaches our standards of conduct.

The UMKL is open only to individuals aged 18 or above who are enrolled in, or alumni/faculty of, higher education institutions in the United Kingdom. This ensures compliance with online safeguarding expectations and reduces institutional risk.

The UMKL is coordinated by a volunteer board made up of students and alumni from across the United Kingdom, who oversee rules, moderation and season management. Participants can report misconduct or concerns about fairness or safety directly to the UMKL Board on Discord. All reports are handled by neutral, non-affiliated board members to ensure equal treatment.

Data Protection and Privacy

The UMKL stores Discord user IDs only for match organisation and point tracking. This is stored securely in accordance with the Data Protection Act (2018), and is not disclosed in any capacity without the consent of the owner.

The privacy policy for the Cheep Cheep Discord App (a 'bot' that stores player data) can be found here.

Discord user IDs and match records are retained for points tracking and results generation only. Access is limited to UMKL administrative volunteers for operational purposes, with the UMKL Board acting as data controller for any information processed during competition. User data may be downloaded at anytime with the /export-my-data command in the Discord server.

Any request to review data privacy and information collected should be submitted to the UMKL Board through our Discord server.

Closing

The UMKL wants every student to have an amazing experience with us, and we can't wait to see you on the track. For queries, feedback or concerns, please contact the UMKL Board through our Discord server.
