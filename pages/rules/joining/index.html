Joining Guide

Information for universities wanting to join the UMKL

Welcome

Joining the University Mario Kart League is a commitment to the future of Mario Kart e-sports in the United Kingdom, and we would love to have your university represented in the league.

Being part of the UMKL is a responsibility. Teams are expected to stay active throughout the season and to act respectfully towards opponents and peers. We reserve the right to remove a team from the league for disruptive behaviour.

The UMKL provides students a glimpse into the ever-growing world of competitive Mario Kart

Note
The UMKL is not affiliated with any university, union or brand, and all university teams are independent. Please read the University Relations page for more information.

Creating a New Team

If you are interested in joining the UMKL, we recommend that you (and other interested players) join the UMKL Discord server first, so that board members can discuss your upcoming team and help you get set up.

Before creating a team, make sure there are enough players at your university who are interested in participating.

  • A team must have a minimum of six players available to play in every match. Line-ups can change between matches based on your roster's availability, so it's recommended to have reserve members who can fill in if players are unavailable
  • University teams should not be tied to a specific society. Anybody at the university should be free to join the team regardless of their affiliation with any tangential Mario Kart or e-sports society
  • Current students, alumni, university faculty and prospective students (holding an offer) are welcome to join your team, but students outside of your university may not be allowed to participate

You'll need a name, team colour and an emblem for your team, read the Team Guidelines for information about this.

Important
Match line-ups and team practice should be discussed within the UMKL server, not a separate society server that some members may not have access to. This keeps the UMKL free and open to everyone.

You're welcome to promote the UMKL through society channels, but must make clear that membership of that society isn't required to join the UMKL team. See the Brand Guidelines page for details on using UMKL intellectual property and content.

Once your team is ready, contact the board with your request to join on the UMKL Discord server. If accepted, your team will be inducted into the following season, which typically runs from October to April.

The board will also ask your team to nominate two individuals to be board members for your team, and who will use the /create-team command to formally create your team and join the UMKL.

Next Steps

You are now ready to join the UMKL! Make sure everyone on your team is familiar with the Match Rules and Streaming Guidelines before applying.

  • Match Rules - the rules for playing matches, everyone must follow these rules
  • Streaming Guidelines - rules and helpful info for people streaming matches
Promoting the UMKL
Be sure to read the Brand Guidelines for information on our brand/identity. It also has assets for help with promoting or posting about the UMKL, for example at freshers fairs or on social media.
